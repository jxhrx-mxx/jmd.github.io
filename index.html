ABOUT ME

Hi! I am JEHRA MEA DELA PEÑA, a Graphic Designer and Developer who has been battle-tested in both the digital and physical workplace. My journey has taken me from the fast-paced world of online lead generation to the technical rigors of a medical laboratory. These experiences have made me more than just a creative; they’ve made me a multidisciplinary problem-solver. I don’t just create visuals that look good—I build digital solutions that are backed by technical precision, clear communication, and a work ethic that thrives on challenges.

MY JOURNEY

My professional path began in the digital space as a Lead Generation Specialist, a role that served as my 'crash course' in the online business world. Over two years, I pushed past my natural introversion to become a resilient, confident communicator, learning how to bridge the gap between a brand and its audience under high-pressure conditions. During this time, I took on a specialized Junior WordPress Developer contract, which was a turning point for me—it’s where I first found my 'flow' by combining technical HTML/CSS logic with my passion for design.Most recently, I transitioned into a high-responsibility onsite role as an IT Staff & Specimen Collector. For the past two years, I’ve balanced the 'logical'—managing hardware, government permits, and sensitive DOH data—with the 'creative'—designing logos and visual assets for the laboratory. This role has solidified my ability to handle complex, high-stakes tasks with the same precision I bring to my creative work.
